Demon Slayer (Kimetsu No Yaiba) – Complete Overview

Demon Slayer is one of the most popular anime in the world. It currently has five seasons, each with its own focus and storyline.

Season 1: The Beginning

The first season was officially released on April 6, 2019, and has 26 episodes.

This season follows Kamado Tanjiro's journey as he tries to turn his sister Nezuko back into a human after their family is slaughtered by demons.

Season 2: Mugen Train ARC

The second season was released in late 2021 to early 2022, with a total of 7 episodes.

Season 2 focuses on the continued fight against powerful demons, including Akaza, the Upper Rank Three of the Twelve Kizuki.

It retells the events of the Mugen Train movie with new scenes and music, giving more depth to the story.

Season 3: Swordsmith Village Arc

The third season was officially released in Japan on April 9, 2023, with 11 episodes.

This season follows Tanjiro's fight to protect the hidden Swordsmith Village. The Demon Slayer Corps works to strengthen their weapons after an attack by two high-ranking demons.

Season 4: Hashira Training Arc

The fourth season aired on May 12, 2024, and has 8 episodes.

This season shows the training of the Hashira (elite demon slayers) as they prepare themselves to fight Muzan Kibutsuji, the main antagonist.

Season 5: Infinity Castle Movie

The fifth season, the movie Infinity Castle, was released in Japan on July 18, 2025. Its runtime is approximately 155 minutes (2 hours 35 minutes).

The movie features fantastic animation and intense battles against the strongest demons, including Akaza and Doma.

Tanjiro and Giyu Tomioka face off against Akaza again in a battle full of unpredictable action.

Events After Training

After his training, Tanjiro had become much stronger and more skilled. In the Infinity Castle, after entering, Tanjiro and Giyu fought together, while others like Zenitsu also used all their strength. They even had to fight old friends who had been turned into demons.

Later, the Butterfly Sister was killed in the Infinity Castle, which was an extremely sad and emotional moment.

Muzan Kibutsuji was incredibly powerful and extremely hard to defeat. His jet-black hair and fierce appearance gave him the look of a true villain. He commands many demons whose job is to attack humans and turn them into demons, as Muzan plans to destroy humanity worldwide.
